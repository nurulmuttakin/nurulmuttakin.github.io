Md Nurul Muttakin

I am a computer science and engineering faculty at Bangladesh University and a research collaborator at Xu Lab, Carnegie Mellon University, with Prof. Min Xu. My research focuses on developing full-stack ML methodologies for complex structural data arising in bioinformatics and computational biology to solve life science problems such as elucidating disease mechanisms, discovering drug targets, and designing de novo drugs.

Earlier, I did my Masters and Bachelors in computer science and engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, where Prof. Md. Saidur Rahman supervised me.

You are cordially welcome to my tiny corner!
